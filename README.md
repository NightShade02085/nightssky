# nightssky
A simple pixel art editor with some new features! Enjoy the ability to have multiple pixel-project tabs and onion skin from ANY SOURCE open at once and IN ADDITION a never-before-seen feature called "Templates"



HOW TO USE: Approach it like any other pixel art software. If you intend on animating your art then just pop the timeline up from the bottom of the screen. I think most of the other buttons are straightforward except for the Templates, Palettes and Custom Onion Skinning features. So, if you need help...

Templates: Used for reference. Similar to onion skinning but just one key diference. You don't need to have your reference in-project. This can help save time when starting new projects to just have the ability to use a previously made template. Here's how it works: You draw something, save as template, name it, then all you have to do is select it in the templates menu and click "Toggle Ghost"

Palettes: To create your own Palette you have two options, you can create one from scratch or extract from a local image. Creating one from scratch is simple and straightforward, you just follow the editor, I'm sure you won't need help. For extracting you can just choose... say a picture of a grassy field, and the program will extract up to 32 colors from that image giving you a great starting palette for drawing your background scenery!

Onion skinning: It looks a little more complicated that regular onion skinning, but if you just leave the controls alone and toggle it on, it will work just fine for you. However, using the extra features requires a little bit more foreknowledge. You just select what project or "tab" you want to onion skin from and then from there you can choose which layer and even frame to skin from as well.
